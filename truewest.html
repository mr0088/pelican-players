THE PELICAN PLAYERS NYC PRESENT

"True West", written by Sam Shepard in 1980, is a play following the sibling rivalry of two estranged brothers as they collaborate on a screenplay in their mother's home while she is away. Alone together in the West, with the pull of the desert ever present and tempers flaring as frustrations grow, the two struggle for power as they attempt to finish the script.
